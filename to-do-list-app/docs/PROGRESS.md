## ğŸ”¬ Research-Backed Productivity Principles to Implement

### 1. **Deep Work (Cal Newport)**
- Uninterrupted focus blocks (90-120 minutes)
- Eliminate shallow work during deep work sessions
- Track deep work hours per day/week

### 2. **Pomodoro Technique (Francesco Cirillo)**
- 25-minute focus sessions
- 5-minute short breaks
- 15-30 minute long breaks after 4 pomodoros

### 3. **Habit Formation (James Clear - Atomic Habits)**
- Make it obvious: Visual cue system
- Make it attractive: Reward system
- Make it easy: Reduce friction
- Make it satisfying: Streak tracking

### 4. **Two-Minute Rule**
- If task takes < 2 minutes, do it immediately
- Flag tasks as "quick wins"

### 5. **Zeigarnik Effect**
- Breaking tasks into smaller chunks
- Progress tracking to reduce mental load

### 6. **Time Boxing**
- Allocate specific time blocks for tasks
- Parkinson's Law: Work expands to fill time

### 7. **Energy Management**
- Track when you're most productive
- Schedule hard tasks during peak energy
- Easy tasks during low energy
